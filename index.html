FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Do I need to change my WhatsApp number?

No. We connect Kwicker AI to your existing business WhatsApp number. Your customers keep messaging the same number they already know.

Will customers know they're talking to an AI?

The AI is trained to match your brand's tone and personality. Most customers can't tell the difference. And if you ever want to step in personally, you can pause the AI and take over the conversation at any time.

What languages does it support?

Kwicker AI works in any language — English, Arabic, Hindi, and more. It automatically matches whatever language the customer is writing in.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are fully set up and live within 24 hours. We handle the technical side — you just tell us about your business, products, and how you like to communicate.

What counts as a "conversation"?

A conversation is a 24-hour window of messaging with one customer. Whether you exchange 3 messages or 30, it counts as one conversation. Follow-up messages sent within the same 24-hour window are included.

Can I change the AI's responses or train it?

Yes. You can update your product catalog, pricing, AI instructions, and tone anytime from the dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

What if I go over my conversation limit?

We'll notify you before you hit your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time — or we can discuss a custom plan that fits your volume.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No contracts. No lock-in. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you into a contract.
