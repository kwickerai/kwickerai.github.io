Kwicker AI — Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Please read this policy carefully.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Kwicker AI ("we", "our", or "us") is a WhatsApp-powered sales CRM for merchants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Kwicker AI mobile application ("App") and related services. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and hashed password when you register as a merchant.
  • Business Information: Your business name and WhatsApp Business number that you connect to the platform.
  • Lead & Customer Data: Phone numbers, names, and conversation messages from customers who interact with your WhatsApp Business account through our platform.
  • Conversation Data: WhatsApp messages exchanged between your customers and the AI assistant, including message content and timestamps.
  • Device Information: Device push notification tokens (FCM) to deliver alerts to your device.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and error logs to improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and operate the Kwicker AI sales CRM service
  • Authenticate and manage your merchant account
  • Power the AI assistant to handle and qualify customer conversations
  • Send push notifications about new leads and conversation activity
  • Display lead pipeline, intent scores, and sales insights within the App
  • Improve and debug the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Processing of Messages

Kwicker AI uses artificial intelligence to process incoming WhatsApp messages on your behalf. Message content is analysed to generate replies, assess customer intent, and categorise leads. This processing is done to fulfil the core service you have subscribed to. AI-generated responses are sent only to your customers via your connected WhatsApp Business account.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business API: To send and receive messages. Subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase: For push notifications and crash reporting. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered message processing and lead qualification. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway (Hosting): Our backend infrastructure provider. Customer data is stored on servers managed by Railway.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. Lead and conversation data is retained to provide pipeline history and insights. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 10). Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed password storage, and access controls on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@kwickerai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Kwicker AI is intended solely for use by business owners and merchants. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete that information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us at info@kwickerai.com.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where our hosting and third-party providers operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
